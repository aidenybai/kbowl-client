Frequently Asked Questions

What is this? kbowl.party is a virtual web application for oral knowledge bowl. kbowl.party is best paired with Zoom for the host narrate questions and manage the queue/scoreboard. We also recommend participants to be muted in the Zoom call, but have another call with their team on platforms like Discord or Facetime (watch the video demo)
Who works on this? kbowl.party was created by Aiden Bai. Feel free to reach out if you have any issues, suggestions, orquestions.
What is the status of kbowl.party? kbowl.party is currently in open beta, meaning that the application may have bugs. Participants should strive to be good samaritans when using kbowl.party.
